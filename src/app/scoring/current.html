• You are likely to need to buy more credit in winter
• If you have sight difficulties or other special needs, this might not be suitable
If there is a debt recovery this also needs to be explained to the customer by agreeing a DRR to be collected via the meter. Advisor should also explain when any arrears will be cleared.
